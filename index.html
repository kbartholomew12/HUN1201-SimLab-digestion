Digestive Traffic Control: Process, Absorb, or Move Forward

Guide one mixed meal through the digestive tract.

At each checkpoint, decide what the digestive system should do next. Your choices change the digestive environment and determine whether nutrients are ready to cross the intestinal border.

The key question throughout the SimLab: Should the body process this meal, absorb its products, or move the remaining material forward?

Criteria

About the dashboard

The four dashboard values are learning indicators, not clinical measurements. Higher values show greater progress toward appropriate particle size, pH conditions, absorbable nutrient forms, and successful transport.
